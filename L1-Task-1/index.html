Arabica Coffee

Arabica beans are by far the most popular type of coffee beans, making up about 60% of the world’s coffee.The downside of Arabica beans is that they’re harder to grow. Because of this, Arabica beans are pricier

Robusta Coffee

Robusta beans are larger and more rounded than the other bean varieties. These plants typically grow much larger than Arabica plants, measuring between 15 and 20 feet.

Liberica Coffee

Coffea liberica is prized for its piquant floral aroma and bold, smoky flavor profile. This hardy species is frequently mixed with other varieties to add body and complexity.
